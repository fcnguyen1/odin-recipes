Hot & Sour Soup


Description: Traditional Hot & Sour Soup - Ashies' Way

Ingredients

  • Dry Red Chili
  • Shitake Mushrooms
  • Ground Pork
  • White pepper
  • Salt
  • Red Chili Flakes
  • Dried Woodear
  • Bamboo Shoots
  • Pickled Mustard Tuber
  • Chicken Broth
  • Hot Sour Soup Mix
  • Soy Sauce
  • White Vinegar
  • Eggs
  • Tofu
  • Green Onions

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a pot. Throw in chilis
  2. Cook shitake mushrooms. Dehydrate woodear
  3. Throw in pork and season with soy sauce, salt, white pepper, and chili. Cook til brown
  4. Pour in chicken broth
  5. Chop woodear, bamboo shoots, mustard tuber and throw into the pot
  6. Bring soup to a boil. Throw in soup mix
  7. Season with vinegar, soy sauce, and chili flakes to taste
  8. Cube tofu and throw in pot
  9. Lightly whisk eggs and slowly pour into boiling soup
  10. Top with chopped green onion and enjoy
