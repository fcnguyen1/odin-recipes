Bacon Chicken Pasta

Description: My favorite pasta that Ashie made for me in college.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Bacon
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Red chili flakes
  • Lemon juice
  • Heavy cream
  • Tomato
  • Spinach
  • Olive Oil
  • Grated Parmesan

Directions

  1. Boil water and salt. Cook pasta til al dente then drain
  2. Heat pan on medium. Cut up bacon and throw in heated pan
  3. Julienne shallots and finely chop the garlic
  4. Drain most of the oil from teh bacon. Throw in shallots and garlic
  5. Cust chicken into small pieces and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and chili flakes
  6. When shallots are cooked, throw in chicken and lemon juice
  7. Dice tomato and trim stems off spinach
  8. When chicken is cooked, pour in cream
  9. Season with salt, pepper , and parmesan cheese to taste
  10. Throw in tomato and spinach
  11. Drizzle olive oil on pasta, pour sauce over and gently combine
  12. Top with parmesan and red chili flaeks and enjoy
