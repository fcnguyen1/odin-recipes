Omurice

Description: My favorite thing to cook while I was alone at Cal Poly.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Dried Red Chilis
  • Asparagus
  • Mushrooms
  • Jalapeno
  • Chinese Sausage
  • Soy Sauce
  • Curry Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Curry Roux
  • Water

Directions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cut onion into strings. Mince garlic. Heat oil in pan on medium and cook till fragrant
  3. Throw in chilis. Cut tender parts of asparagus into one inch pieces. Throw into pan
  4. Slice mushrooms and cut jalapeno into long slices. Throw in
  5. Slice sausage and throw in
  6. Splash with soy sauce. Season with curry powder and pepper
  7. Add rice and mix with veggies and sausage. Season with more soy sauce and curry powder
  8. Flatten rice in pan. Turn heat to high for 1-2 minutes then turn heat off and let bottom crisp. Move rice onto place
  9. Break eggs into oiled pan on low heat. Stir in the pan with chopsticks til lightly scrambled. Before completely cooked, start folding over edges. Flip omelete on top of rice
  10. Dissolve curry roux in hot water. Pour over omelete and enjoy
